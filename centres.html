Centres

Our five community centres

Each centre provides a welcoming space for different kinds of support, activities, and community connection.

Centre 01

Main Support Hub

General enquiries, first contact support, food relief information, and referrals to other local services.

Centre 02

Community Kitchen Centre

Shared meal services, food connection activities, and a warm space for people to meet and feel welcome.

Centre 03

Skills and Sewing Studio

Creative and practical workshops that support learning, confidence building, and personal development.

Centre 04

Neighbourhood Meeting Space

Community gatherings, family sessions, support groups, and local information events.

Centre 05

Outreach and Volunteer Desk

Volunteer coordination, partnership enquiries, outreach planning, and community engagement activities.
